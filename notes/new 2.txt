Copyrighted Books (Controlled Lending)
For more modern books that are still protected by copyright, the Archive uses a "Controlled Digital Lending" model:

Account Required: You must sign up for a free Internet Archive account to borrow these books.

Borrowing Limits: You can typically borrow a book for 1 hour (renewable) or 14 days.

Waitlists: Because the Archive generally only lends out as many digital copies as they have physical copies in storage, you may have to join a waitlist if all copies are currently checked out.

Reading Formats: You can read them instantly using the online "BookReader" in your browser or download them to encrypted files (DRM) that require specific software like Adobe Digital Editions or LCP-compliant readers (e.g., Thorium).
